The Equilibrium & Computation Reading Group is a discussion group organized during Simons Workshop on Learning and Games to study areas like min-max optimization, disequilibrium phenomena, smoothed analysis in games & learning, equlibrium notions and multi-agent reinforcement learning. We concentrate on readings that apply nice analytic or computational models to questions in AGT & ML. We emphasize both on Game theoretic solution concepts and the impact of the structure on learning dynamics. During the Spring term of 2022 we met on a weekly basis to present and discuss important papers in the field.
